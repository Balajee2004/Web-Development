Key Themes and Summary

Date: 23.11.2023 and 24.11.2023

Synopsis :

A key objective of the International Conference on Applied AI and its Futuristic Applications is to explore the changing potential of artificial intelligence (AI) in various sectors that are critical to our society's development. Among the topics to be discussed will be agriculture, autonomous vehicles, education, egovernment, energy, national security, healthcare, and the Indian judicial system. Participants from around the world will share knowledge, discuss emerging trends, and exchange ideas about how AI can be harnessed for a better future. A keynote address will be given by renowned experts in AI and its applications at the opening ceremony. There will be a series of presentations on AI technology advancements and its impact on the various sectors. There will be a lively and informative conference program following the ceremony.

Key Themes :

Agriculture :

•AI-based crop monitoring and yield prediction.

●Precision agriculture and smart farming techniques.

●Drone technology for agricultural surveillance and crop spraying.

●AI-powered pest and disease detection in crops.

●Intelligent irrigation and water management systems.

Autonomous Vehicles :

●AI algorithms for autonomous navigation and obstacle detection.

●Machine learning in intelligent transportation systems.

●Safety and ethical considerations in autonomous vehicle development.

●AI-enabled predictive maintenance for vehicles Connected and self-driving vehicles in smart cities.

Education :

●Adaptive learning systems using AI.

●AI-powered virtual tutors and personalized education platforms.

●Automated grading and feedback mechanisms intelligent educational data analytics and insights.

●AI-based tools for education accessibility and inclusion.

E-Governance :

●AI applications in government service delivery and citizen engagement.

●AI-driven decision support systems for policymaking.

●Data analytics and predictive modelling for effective governance.

●AI-enabled fraud detection and cybersecurity in public services.

●Ethical and legal implications of AI in e-governance.

Energy :

●AI-based energy management and optimization.

●Smart grid technologies and AI-driven energy distribution.

●Predictive maintenance in energy infrastructure.

●Energy consumption analysis and efficiency enhancement using AI.

●Renewable energy forecasting and integration with the grid.

National Security :

●AI for threat detection and cybersecurity.

●Surveillance systems using AI and computer vision Predictive analytics for crime prevention and counterterrorism.

●AI-enabled border security and intelligent surveillance Ethical considerations in AI-powered national security systems.

Healthcare :

●AI applications in disease diagnosis and medical imaging.

●Predictive analytics for early disease detection and personalized treatment.

●AI-driven drug discovery and precision medicine.

●Robotics and AI in surgery and healthcare operations.

●Privacy, security, and ethical challenges in AI-enabled healthcare.

Indian Judicial System :

●AI-assisted legal research and case analysis Natural language processing for legal document processing.

●AI-based tools for legal decision support and analysis Predictive analytics in legal proceedings and case management.

●Ethical and fairness considerations in AI-driven legal systemsd

Summary.

Participants will be able to share their research findings, best practices, and innovative solutions during plenary sessions, panel discussions, paper presentations, and poster sessions at the conference. In addition, AI applications in the designated sectors will be showcased and demonstrated. Ultimately, the International Conference on Applied AI and its Futuristic Applications in Agriculture, Autonomous Vehicles, Education Systems, E-Governance, Energy, National Security, Healthcare, and Indian Judicial System will provide an opportunity for ideas to be exchanged, innovations showcased, and collaborations to explore the role of AI in solving global challenges and shaping a brighter future.
